Lightweight Abrasive Hose

MATERIAL HANDLING

Product Overview

A lightweight, flexible hose designed for air suction and delivery in abrasive applications. Its corrugated construction ensures high flexibility, making it perfect for woodworking shops, industrial cleaning, and fume extraction.

  • High flexibility and lightweight
  • Good abrasion resistance
  • Wire helix for suction capability
  • Corrugated cover for ease of handling

Technical Specifications

TubeNatural Rubber (NR), Black, smooth.
ReinforcementHigh tensile synthetic cord with steel wire helix.
CoverBlack, corrugated, weather resistant.
Working Pressure5 Bar
Vacuum0.8 Bar
Temperature-30°C to +70°C
