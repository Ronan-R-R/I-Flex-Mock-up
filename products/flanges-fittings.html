Product Overview

L-Flex supplies a comprehensive range of end connections for our hoses. We offer both built-in (vulcanised) fittings for high-pressure integrity and clamped fittings for field repairability. We cater to all major standards including Table D, ANSI, and DIN.

  • Mild Steel and Stainless Steel options
  • Vulcanised (Integral) or Swaged options
  • Custom machining available
  • Complete assemblies provided

Available Types

FlangesTable D, Table E, 10/3, 16/3, ANSI 150, DIN.
CouplingsCamlocks, Bauer Couplings, Claw Couplings.
ThreadedBSP, NPT (Male/Female).
MaterialsMild Steel, Stainless Steel (304/316), Brass, Aluminum.
