Pinch Valves

VALVES & CONTROLS

Product Overview

Pinch valves are the ideal solution for controlling abrasive and corrosive slurries. The valve features a rubber sleeve which is the only wetted part, protecting the mechanical components from the medium. When closed, it provides a 100% tight seal.

  • Full bore flow (No pressure drop)
  • No clogging or mechanical blockage
  • Replaceable rubber sleeve
  • Available in manual or actuated operation

Technical Specifications

Body MaterialCast Iron, Aluminum, or Fabricated Steel.
Sleeve MaterialNatural Rubber, EPDM, Neoprene, etc.
OperationManual Handwheel, Pneumatic, or Hydraulic.
Pressure RatingUp to 16 Bar depending on size.
Size Range50mm to 300mm+
