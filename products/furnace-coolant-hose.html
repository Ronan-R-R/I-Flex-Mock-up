Furnace Coolant Hose

SPECIALTY

Product Overview

Designed specifically for the rigorous environment of steel mills and foundries. This hose conveys cooling water to furnace doors and ladle covers. It features a specialized non-conductive construction to prevent electrical arcing and an exterior designed to resist high radiant heat.

  • Electrically non-conductive (Carbon-free)
  • Resists heat and splashes of molten metal
  • Glass fiber cover options available
  • Flame retardant
