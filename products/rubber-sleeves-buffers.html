Product Overview

We manufacture a wide variety of custom rubber sleeves and buffers. Sleeves act as flexible connectors between rigid pipes or chutes, allowing for movement and vibration. Buffers are designed to absorb impact and protect machinery.

  • Custom shapes and sizes
  • High impact absorption (Buffers)
  • Dust sealing (Sleeves)
  • Vibration isolation
