Hose Floats

MARINE & DREDGING

Product Overview

Designed to keep dredging and discharge hoses afloat on the water surface. Our floats are made from highly durable, bright materials for visibility and impact resistance. They are essential for marine dredging and dam dewatering operations.

  • Rotomoulded Polyethylene shell
  • High visibility Orange color
  • Easy assembly with galvanized bolts
  • Resistant to impact and weather

Technical Specifications

MaterialPolyethylene outer shell, Foam filled (optional).
DesignTwo halves bolted together around the hose.
SizesTo suit hose IDs from 50mm to 300mm+.
BuoyancyCalculated to support specific hose and slurry weights.
