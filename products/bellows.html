Bellows & Expansion Joints

COMPONENTS

Product Overview

Rubber bellows (expansion joints) are designed to absorb thermal expansion, contraction, and vibration in piping systems. They protect pumps and valves from stress and noise, extending the lifespan of your installation.

  • Compensates for axial and lateral movement
  • Reduces noise and vibration
  • Wide range of rubber compounds available
  • Single or multi-arch designs

Technical Specifications

Body MaterialEPDM, NBR, Neoprene, Hypalon.
ReinforcementNylon fabric with steel wire.
FlangesFloating (Swivel) flanges in mild steel.
Pressure10 Bar / 16 Bar
VacuumResistant with internal vacuum rings.
