Pulp & Slurry Hose

MINING & INDUSTRIAL

Product Overview

Our Pulp and Slurry hose is hand-built to withstand the harshest mining environments. It features a specialized abrasive-resistant liner ranging from 6mm to 24mm depending on customer requirements.

  • High abrasion resistance for long service life
  • Custom lengths available up to 20m
  • Available with built-in flanges (fixed or swivel)
  • UV and weather resistant cover

Technical Specifications

TubeBlack, smooth, abrasion-resistant NR/SBR rubber compound.
ReinforcementHigh tensile synthetic fabric with embedded steel wire helix.
CoverBlack, corrugated (wrapped finish), weather and ozone resistant.
Temperature Range-40°C to +70°C
Liner Thickness6mm to 24mm (Customizable)
Working Pressure10 Bar (Standard) - High pressure options available.
