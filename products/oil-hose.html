Oil Hose

INDUSTRIAL TRANSFER

Product Overview

Engineered for the safe transfer of petroleum-based products, diesel, and oils. This hose is built with an oil-resistant NBR lining to prevent swelling and degradation, making it ideal for refineries, depots, and mobile tankers.

  • Oil resistant tube (High NBR content)
  • Weather and ozone resistant cover
  • Flexible at low temperatures
  • Available in various diameters

Technical Specifications

TubeBlack, smooth, NBR (Nitrile) rubber.
ReinforcementHigh tensile textile cord.
CoverBlack, smooth or wrapped, NBR/EPDM blend.
Working Pressure10 Bar / 20 Bar options.
Safety Factor3:1
Temperature-30°C to +100°C
