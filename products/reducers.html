Product Overview

Rubber reducers are critical for connecting pipes or hoses of different diameters. Unlike steel reducers, rubber reducers absorb vibration and accommodate slight misalignments, protecting the pump and pipeline from stress.

  • Concentric or Eccentric designs available
  • Absorbs noise and vibration
  • Abrasion resistant inner liner options
  • Custom lengths and flange drillings

Technical Specifications
