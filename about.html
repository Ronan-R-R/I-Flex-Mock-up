About L-Flex

Over 30 years of excellence in rubber hose manufacturing.

Our Story

L-Flex was established in 1993, manufacturing rubber hoses and providing technical service in product design. Over the years, we have been actively involved in the Rubber Industry in various disciplines and technical work studies.

This deep involvement has afforded us an experiential advantage in Material Handling and Product Application. We don't just sell hoses; we understand the specific pressures, abrasives, and environmental conditions they face in the field.

Our Mission

To supply tailor-made solutions for your rubber hose requirements with a focus on quality, safety, and rapid delivery. We believe in building relationships as strong and durable as our products.

Our Quality Promise

Every hose that leaves our Benoni facility is hand-built to rigorous standards. We utilize premium rubber compounds (NR, SBR, EPDM, NBR) and high-tensile reinforcement to ensure safety factors of 3:1 for general use and higher for critical applications like concrete pumping.
