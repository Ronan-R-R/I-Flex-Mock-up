About L-Flex

Over 30 years of excellence in rubber hose manufacturing.

Our Story

L-Flex was established in 1993, manufacturing rubber hoses and providing technical service in product design. Over the years, we have been actively involved in the Rubber Industry in various disciplines and technical work studies.

This has afforded us an experiential advantage in Material Handling and Product Application, allowing us to deliver solutions that last longer in the field.

Our Mission

To supply tailor-made solutions for your rubber hose requirements with a focus on quality, safety, and rapid delivery.
